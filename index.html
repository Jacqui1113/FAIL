The title "Eternal Sunshine" evokes a sense of both longing and nostalgia, reflecting the themes explored in both Ariana Grande's album and the Charlie Kaufman film. In Grande's album, the title suggests a desire for a fresh start, a chance to erase painful memories and move forward. Similarly, in Kaufman's film, the protagonist undergoes a procedure to erase his memories of a failed relationship, hoping to find peace and closure. Both works delve into the complex nature of memory and its enduring impact on our lives.

Now, what you are about to see there is pretty similar and has a bond with what both Grande and Kaufman wanted us to feel.
